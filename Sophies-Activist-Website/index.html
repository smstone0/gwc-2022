We are the EV Advocates

What do we do?

We support the widespread adoption of Electric Vehicles (EVs) by providing a platform and a voice for the public to advocate for change in order to accelerate progress. We also provide information on the benefits EVs bring to society, as well as the limitations in progression of EV adoption.

Why do we do this?

It is extremely important to us to encourage a sustainable future and EVs bring many benefits to society. Learn more by viewing our EV Benefits page. It is also important to us to spread awareness on the limitations in progression. Learn more by viewing our Limitations page.

How can I get involved?

We are glad that you would like to get involved! Anyone can make an impact and there is strength in numbers. Please view our Take Action page for more information on how you can make a difference.
